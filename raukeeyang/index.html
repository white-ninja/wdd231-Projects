About Raukeeyang Integrated Farm

Raukeeyang Integrated Farm is a modern livestock and food production farm dedicated to sustainable agriculture, healthy animal rearing, and supplying fresh farm produce to homes, businesses, and markets across Nigeria.

We combine innovation with traditional farming values to ensure quality, food safety, and environmental responsibility in everything we produce.
